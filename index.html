Coming Back

Many of us dropped the board after finishing school or college but still feel the itch to skate. Plenty have either lost touch with the people they used to skate with or are the only one that wants to skate again. If you are of like mind then sign up now and connect with others.

Start Now!

Looking to start? Our meetups are chill and without judgement. If you have a desire to learn we have motivated skaters that love to see people learn a new trick just as much as learning it themselves. The best time to start skating was in your teenage years. The second best time is right now! Stop putting off what you want to do and sign up now!

Make Friends

Skating is a great way to make new friends. Hanging out for hours trying new tricks or just sitting around the skate-park after a session talking about anything. In todays online world it can be a daunting experience trying to make new friends thats why we are running it back to simplier times. Common interests are common grounds and make for brilliant ice breakers so sign up now and lets skate!.

Get Fit

An often overlooked part of skateboarding is the benefits on health. Skateboarding is not only a great cardio workout its also an extremely affordable hobby. It improves coordination and strenght and can even be a great mode of transport if you are city living. It also drills in how to fall safely

Wear a Helmet!

Here at Aging Skater we stronlgy encourage the use of helmets. We only get the one noggin so its best to take care of it. We aren't teenagers anymore so it doesn't matter if you look "uncool"
We stronly recommend that all ages of skaters keep their helmets on.

Learn at your pace

Aging Skater likes to keep a relaxed atmosphere when we meet up so don't be afraid to come with no experience. Learn at your own pace with the othe skaters and don't worry about keeping up.
